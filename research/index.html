1. Customized Neural Network Design⌗

Research in this area centers on the design and development of customized neural network architectures tailored to a wide range of domain-specific applications. The focus is on building models that are not only accurate and robust but also lightweight, interpretable, and efficient‚Äîenabling practical deployment in real-world, resource-constrained environments.
Suitable roles: AI/ML Engineer, AI/ML Researcher

2. Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI)⌗

A major theme of our recent work involves the pursuit of explainable artificial intelligence (XAI), which aims to enhance the transparency of AI decision-making processes. By offering insights into why and how models make decisions, XAI facilitates the development of systems that are easier to adapt and optimize across different devices, scenarios, and data distributions, thereby improving both operational efficiency and generalization capabilities.
Suitable roles: AI Researcher, Data Scientist, Model Optimization Engineer

3. Databases and Data Mining⌗

This line of research focuses on the development of efficient algorithms and systems for managing and analyzing large-scale datasets. Key efforts include optimizing similarity search, query processing, and real-time data retrieval. In parallel, we leverage advanced data mining techniques to uncover meaningful patterns and actionable insights from complex, high-dimensional data.
Suitable roles: Algorithm Engineer, Data Scientist, Big Data Engineer

4. Software Systems Development⌗

We build robust software systems that integrate our models and algorithms to support practical applications across a variety of domains. These systems provide interactive tools and automated frameworks for monitoring, prediction, and decision support. Many of these systems are developed in collaboration with domain experts and industry partners, including National Taiwan University Hospital and Vision Wide Tech, to ensure real-world relevance and validated impact. Suitable roles: Software Engineer, System Integration Engineer, Full Stack Developer

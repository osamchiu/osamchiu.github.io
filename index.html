📖 Biography

Sheng-Min Sam Chiu received his B.S. degree in Information Engineering and Computer Science from Feng Chia University in 2017, and his M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science and Information Engineering from National Cheng Kung University in 2019 and 2023, respectively. From May 2022 to April 2023, he was a visiting scholar at the School of Computing, National University of Singapore. He is currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronic Engineering at National United University, and will be joining the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at National Sun Yat-sen University as an Assistant Professor in August 2025. His recent research interests include AI/ML, feature Selection, smart manufacturing, and spatio-temporal databases.
